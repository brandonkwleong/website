I'm a developer

I've always loved technology. At a young age you could find me building lego contraptions or tinkering with computers. That love led me to study Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo (in Canada). The program required me to do internships which allowed me to travel North America, interning at companies in Toronto, California and New York.

During my studies in Waterloo, I discovered the my passion for programming. Today I design, write and produce code that people use and I continuously look for ways to improve my craft. I now currently work at Bloomberg LP as a financial software developer.

I'm... other things too
